Blademaster Earthwarder Shaman
Earthwarder Shaman
Calls upon the forces of nature to protect himself and his allies.
Tank
Primary Stat: Strength
Prefered Weapon
One-handed Axes, One-handed Maces, Daggers, Fist Weapons, and Shields
Armor Skills
Passive
Shaman can wear cloth, leather and mail armor and use shields. You receive a 5% increase to your primary stat for wearing mail.

Resources

Mana
The base resource. It recharges over time.

Mastery

Elemental Warding
Passive
Reduces magical damage taken by 15%.

Abilities

Level 10
Rockbiter Weapon
Instant cast
Imbue your weapon with the element of Earth for 1 hour, increasing all threat generation by 30% and reducing damage taken by 5%.
Unleash Earth
Instant cast
Unleashes the Rockbiter enchantment upon the Shaman's weapon, forcing the target enemy to attack the Shaman for 5 sec.
Shamanistic Rage
Instant cast
Reduces all damage taken by 30% for 15 sec. This spell is usable while stunned.
Level 11
Magma Strike
Instant cast
Charges your weapon with magma and burns your target, dealing X Fire damage and Y Fire damage over 5s.
Level 18
Cleanse Spirit
Instant cast
Removes all Curse effects from a friendly target.
Level 20
Earth Shield
Instant cast
Protects the target with an earthen shield, increasing your healing on them by 20% and healing them for X when they take damage. This heal can only occur once every few seconds. Maximum 9 charges.
Earth Shield can only be placed on one target at a time. Only one Elemental Shield can be active on the Shaman at a time.
Level 27
Stone Bulwark Totem
Instant cast
Summons an Earth Totem with 10% of the caster's health at the feet of the caster for 30 sec that grants the caster a shield absorbing X damage for 10 sec, and up to an additional Y every 5 sec thereafter.
Level 29
Riptide
Instant cast
Restorative waters wash over a friendly target, healing them for X and an additional Y over 18 sec.
Level 34
Earthen Wall Totem
Instant cast
Summons a totem with X health at your feet for 15 sec. Y damage from each attack against allies within 10 yards of the totem is redirected to the totem.
Level 38
Fire Nova
Instant cast
Ignites your Flame Shock spell on up to 6 nearby enemies, causing each of them to emit a wave of flames that deals X Fire damage to every other enemy within 10 yards.
Level 44
Spirit Walk
Instant cast
Removes all movement impairing effects and increases your movement speed by 60% for 8 sec.
Level 49
Sand Elemental
Instant cast
Calls forth a Greater Sand Elemental to damage the Shaman's enemies.
