Tinker

Ingenious Minds

Tinkers are among the smartest adventurers of Azeroth and, thanks to their incredible inventions, they can overcome nearly any situation. Whether with healing guns or ashtonishing mechanical armors, they're dedicated to aiding their allies in the field. And when all else fails, a powerful explosion is always highly effective.

Class Information

Tinkers possess both intellect and an ample array of phlogiston powered devices to fulfill their role, defending their companions from hurt with their powerful steam armors, healing their wounds with medical contraptions, or defeating their foes with advanced weaponry.
